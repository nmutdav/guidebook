Photo Call Sheet: Cabaret Theatre Production

Photographer: [Name]

Schedule:

  • Monday, 9/30 – Dress Rehearsal: 7:30 PM - 10:00 PM (Brainstorm & Capture Key Shots)
  • Wednesday, 10/2 – Dress Rehearsal: 7:30 PM - 10:00 PM (Take Final Photos)
  • Photo Delivery: Submit 5 Best Photos via USB on Wednesday, 10/2. Deliver Remaining Photos at a later time.

Objective

Capture the essence of the Cabaret production, focusing on key moments, lighting, and emotional expression. The goal is to photograph performers in dynamic, captivating scenes that highlight the mood and visual storytelling of the show.

Shot Ideas for Monday, 9/30 (Brainstorm Key Shots)

  • Opening Act/Introduction
    • Wide-angle shot of the full stage with cast introduction.
    • Close-ups of individual performer expressions.
  • Character Interactions
    • Two-shots of characters during dialogue or tense moments.
    • Over-the-shoulder shots capturing reactions.
  • Lighting & Atmosphere
    • Focus on stage lighting and shadow play.
    • Silhouettes using backlighting.
  • Dance/Choreography
    • Action shots of choreographed movements.
    • Wide shots of group dance numbers.
  • Iconic Cabaret Imagery
    • Close-up of a performer at a microphone with dim lighting.
    • Shots of performers tipping their hats.
  • Set Pieces & Props
    • Close-ups of key props and set details.
    • In-action shots with props (pouring drinks, toasts, etc.).
  • Costume Details
    • Close-up shots of costume elements (sequins, hats, makeup).
  • Dramatic Moments
    • Close-ups during high-tension or emotional scenes.
    • Pauses for dramatic effect.
  • Audience Interaction (if applicable)
    • Capture moments where performers interact with the audience.
  • Curtain Call & Applause
    • Wide shot of the full cast during the curtain call.
    • Crowd reactions, if visible.

Shot Ideas for Wednesday, 10/2 (Final Photos)

  • Refine or recreate shots from Monday’s rehearsal that worked well.
  • Pay attention to lighting and framing, ensuring perfection.

Photo Submission

Wednesday, 10/2: Submit 5 of your best photos via USB to [Recipient]. Ensure a variety of scenes, emotions, and styles are represented. Deliver the remaining photos at a later time.

Important Notes

  • Wear dark clothing to blend in during the rehearsal.
  • Move quietly and discreetly, especially during intense scenes.
  • Ensure you have backup equipment (batteries, memory cards, lenses).
