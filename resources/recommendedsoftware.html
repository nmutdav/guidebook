Software

Free/Open Source

  • DarkTable: A powerful open-source photo editing software with advanced features and an intuitive interface.
  • Audacity: A versatile, free audio editor with a wide range of tools for recording, editing, and mixing audio.
  • DaVinci Resolve: Comprehensive video editing and color correction software with professional features available for free.
  • Processing: A flexible software sketchbook and a language for learning how to code within the context of the visual arts.
  • SuperCollider: An open-source platform for audio synthesis and algorithmic composition, ideal for experimental sound design.
  • VS Code: A modern code editor with built-in support for multiple programming languages and a rich ecosystem of extensions.
  • Open Broadcaster Software (OBS): A free and open-source tool for video recording and live streaming, known for its flexibility and robustness.

University License

  • QLab (MacOS Only): Industry-standard software for creating complex sound and media cues for live performances and installations.
  • Adobe Creative Suite: A suite of professional tools for creative work, including video editing, photo editing, and graphic design.
    • Premiere Pro: A leading video editing software with powerful tools for creating high-quality video content.
    • Lightroom: A robust photo editing and management tool that helps you organize, edit, and share your photos with ease.

Individual License

  • Max: A visual programming language for music and multimedia, allowing for the creation of interactive audio and visual experiences.
  • Ableton Live: A popular digital audio workstation designed for music production and live performance, offering a unique workflow and extensive features.

Accounts

Free or Free Version Available

  • Github.io: A platform for hosting and sharing code repositories, ideal for version control and collaboration.
  • Frame.io: A cloud-based video collaboration tool that facilitates easy sharing and review of video files with teams.
  • Pexels.com: A free stock photo and video site offering high-quality, royalty-free media for creative projects.
  • FreeSound.org: A collaborative database of Creative Commons licensed audio samples, useful for sound design and multimedia projects.
