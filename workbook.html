Welcome to The TDAV Workbook!

In an effort to streamline our task management and improve communication, we've implemented a new system using Google Sheets. This will help us stay organized and minimize the need for constant emails and texts.

Here’s how to use the workbook:

  • Kanban Board
    • Purpose: General task list.
    • Action: Check for new tasks assigned to you when you clock in for shop hours.
  • Scrum Board
    • Purpose: Production task list.
    • Action: Use this for tracking tasks in progress during a theatre production (1-2 week sprints).
  • Inventory
    • Purpose: Track inventory tasks.
    • Action: These tasks are lower priority and can be done once other tasks are complete.

Task Handling:

  • Crew Tasks:
    • Details: Require more than one person (e.g., lifting, moving equipment).
    • Action: Follow our Safety Training Guidelines.
  • “Anyone” Tasks:
    • Details: Do not require multiple people.
    • Action: Complete these when other tasks are finished.

Using the Workbook:

  • Filters:
    • You can filter tasks by due date or by the person assigned to organize information according to your preferences.
  • Status Updates:
    • Change task status from "not started" to "completed" when done.
    • Use "in progress" to indicate ongoing tasks.

Additional Notes:

  • You do not need to check the workbook outside of work hours. For AV emergencies, I will handle them and log the details afterward.
  • This system is new, so don't worry about getting everything perfect right away. If you have any questions or suggestions, please let me know. We might transition to a more advanced platform in the future, but for now, let's make the most of Google Sheets.

Thanks for your cooperation!
