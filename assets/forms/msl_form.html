Marquette Sound Library - Submission Form

Please fill out the form to submit your audio sample for consideration in the Marquette Sound Library. Your sample must meet the following guidelines:

  • File type: WAV or FLAC
  • Sample rate: 48 kHz or higher
  • Bit depth: 24-bit or higher
  • Mono or Stereo
  • File Size: No greater than 290 MB
  • Frequency range: 30 Hz to 10 kHz, peak level no louder than 70 dB

The name, email, and photo associated with your Google account will be recorded when you upload files and submit this form.

By submitting this form, you confirm that the sample provided is your original work. This work will be freely available for download on the Marquette Sound Library and licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution CC BY license. This means the content may be used in both commercial and non-commercial projects but cannot be repackaged and sold elsewhere by anyone, including you.
