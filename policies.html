Nord Piano Policies

The total cost of our Nord Piano Purchase is well over $12,000. Please follow these policies to ensure that our equipment remains in good working order.

Storage

  • Nord Piano #1: Will be set up permanently in the Musical Director's Office for practice.
  • Nord Piano #2: Will be used for performances, and will be stored (not set up) in its case, in the Sound & Media Supervisor's Office.

Usage

Who may use them?

  • The Nord pianos may only be used by the Musical Director for NMU Theatre Productions, until otherwise noted.
  • Rentals are available upon specific request.

Rental Information

Rental Charges:

5% of the total cost x the number of days requested

Example: Nord Piano x1 (pedal, bench, & accessories): $12,271.62 x 0.05 x 1 day = $613.58 per day.

Student and Faculty/Staff Usage

May students use or request to use these pianos?

  • No.
  • Students may request to use the Roland FP30X or the Yamaha MFX pianos.

May faculty/staff request to use these pianos?

  • No. These pianos are explicitly for Musical Performances and Rentals
  • Faculty/staff may request to use the Roland FP30X or the Yamaha MFX pianos.

Damages Policy

Responsibility:

Users are responsible for any damages to the Nord pianos during their usage. This includes accidental damages, wear and tear beyond normal usage, or any other forms of harm caused to the equipment.

Reporting Damages:

Any damages should be reported immediately to the Sound & Media Supervisor. Failure to report damages promptly may result in additional charges.

Repair Costs:

Repair costs will be assessed based on the extent of the damage. Users will be liable for the cost of repairs or replacements required. A detailed invoice will be provided for any repairs, and payment will be expected within 30 days of the invoice date.

Preventative Measures:

Please handle all equipment with care and follow the guidelines for use to prevent damage. Ensure that the pianos are returned to their designated storage locations and properly maintained.
