TH-242: Media and Sound Design - Assignment 7

Midterm Project Proposal

Due: Class 10

Objective: Develop and submit a proposal for your midterm project based on chosen qualities.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Choose Qualities:

    Select three qualities from the provided list. These qualities will guide the theme and design of your midterm project. Examples of qualities include abstract, nostalgic, futuristic, etc.

  2. Outline Your Proposal:

    Create an outline for your midterm project incorporating the chosen qualities. Your proposal should include:

    • Concept: Describe the overall idea or theme of your project.
    • Integration: Explain how each chosen quality will be integrated into your project.
    • Features: Outline the key features you plan to include (e.g., visuals, audio elements, interactivity).
    • Initial Version: Include an initial version of your project demonstrating basic features related to the chosen qualities.

  3. Create Your Proposal Document:

    Prepare a document or presentation detailing your project proposal. Ensure it covers all aspects listed in the outline.

  4. Submit Your Proposal:

    • Submit your proposal document or presentation by the due date.
    • Include a link or attachment for the initial version of your project.
