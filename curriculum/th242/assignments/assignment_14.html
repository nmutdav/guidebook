TH-242: Media and Sound Design - Assignment 14

Final Project Review

Due: Class 16

Objective: Review and finalize your project based on peer and instructor feedback.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Review Feedback:

    Carefully review the feedback received on your final project from both peers and the instructor. Identify key areas for improvement and make note of specific suggestions.

  2. Make Necessary Adjustments:

    Incorporate the feedback into your project. Focus on making meaningful improvements that enhance the overall quality and effectiveness of your work.

  3. Document Changes:

    Prepare a summary of the changes you made based on the feedback. Include details on how these adjustments have improved your project.

  4. Submit Your Final Project:

    • Submit the finalized version of your project by the due date.
    • Include a summary of the changes made and how they address the feedback received.
    • Submit your Processing sketch along with your reflection via email to the course instructor at dmrakovc@nmu.edu with Subject: TH242 - Assignment 14.
