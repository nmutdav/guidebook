TH-242: Media and Sound Design - Assignment 8

Midterm Project Feedback

Due: Class 11

Objective: Review and refine your midterm project based on feedback.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Review Feedback:

    Carefully review the feedback you received on your midterm project. This feedback may come from peers, instructors, or both.

  2. Identify Areas for Improvement:

    Based on the feedback, identify specific areas of your project that need improvement. Focus on aspects such as design, functionality, or content.

  3. Make Revisions:

    Refine your midterm project to address the identified areas for improvement. Make the necessary adjustments to enhance the overall quality of your project.

  4. Prepare Your Revised Project:

    Ensure that your revised project includes the improvements based on the feedback. It should reflect the changes made and showcase a more polished version.

  5. Submit Your Revised Project:

    • Submit the updated version of your midterm project by the due date.
    • Include a brief summary of the feedback received and the changes made based on that feedback.
    • Submit your Processing sketch along with your reflection via email to the course instructor at dmrakovc@nmu.edu with Subject: TH242 - Assignment 8.
