Processing is a powerful tool for both visual and audio design, providing a rich environment for creativity and experimentation. By offering a flexible and accessible platform, Processing enables you to create complex visual art, animations, and interactive applications with ease. Whether you're working on a new sound design project, developing interactive media, or experimenting with generative art, Processing can significantly enhance your creative process.

This guide introduces Processing's capabilities. To fully leverage its potential and expand your skills, independent study and exploration are essential. Your assignments and projects will benefit from a deeper understanding of Processing's diverse features and their applications in design and media.

Explore the following resources to continue your learning journey:

  • Processing Reference - Official documentation and reference for Processing functions and syntax.
  • Processing Tutorials - A collection of tutorials to help you get started and advance your skills.
  • Processing GitHub Repository - Explore the source code and contribute to the Processing project.
  • YouTube Tutorials - Find video tutorials and walkthroughs to see Processing in action.
  • Processing Forum - Join discussions, ask questions, and share your projects with the Processing community.
