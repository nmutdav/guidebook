TH-242 Introduction to Sound & Media Design

Welcome to the "Introduction to Sound & Media Design" course, where you'll explore the fundamentals of sound and media design using both Processing and SuperCollider. This course combines practical, hands-on projects with tutorials to help you master key concepts in multimedia creation. Through Processing, you'll engage with dynamic animations and interactive visuals, while SuperCollider will guide you in designing and manipulating sound. Below, you'll find links to essential course materials, assignments, and resources to support your journey in creating innovative multimedia projects.
