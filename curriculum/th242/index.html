TH-242 Introduction to Sound & Media Design

Welcome to the "Introduction to Sound & Media Design" course. In this class, we will explore the fundamentals of sound and media design using the Processing IDE. This course focuses on leveraging Processing to create dynamic animations and interactive media projects. Through practical, hands-on assignments, you'll develop skills in integrating audio and visual elements, enhancing your understanding of multimedia design.
