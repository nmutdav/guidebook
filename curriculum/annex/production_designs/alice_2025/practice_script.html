Alice in Wonderland - Audition Script

Alice

"Curiouser and curiouser!"

"Oh, dear! I shall be late!"

"Who in the world am I? Ah, that's the great puzzle."

Mad Hatter

"Have I gone mad? I'm afraid so, but let me tell you something, the best people usually are."

"Why is a raven like a writing desk?"

"It's always tea-time, and we've no time to wash the things between whiles."

Cheshire Cat

"We're all mad here."

"You may have noticed that I'm not all there myself."

"Imagination is the only weapon in the war against reality."

Queen of Hearts

"Off with their heads!"

"Who’s been painting my roses red?"

"Sentence first—verdict afterwards."

White Rabbit

"I'm late! I'm late! For a very important date!"

"Oh my fur and whiskers! I'm late, I'm late, I'm late!"

"The hurrier I go, the behinder I get."

Caterpillar

"Who are you?"

"Keep your temper."

"One side will make you grow taller, and the other side will make you grow shorter."

March Hare

"Twinkle, twinkle, little bat! How I wonder what you're at!"

"I dare say you never even spoke to Time!"

"Then you should say what you mean."

Dormouse

"You might just as well say, that 'I breathe when I sleep' is the same thing as 'I sleep when I breathe!'"

"Twinkle, twinkle, twinkle, twinkle..."

"You can draw water out of a water-well..."
